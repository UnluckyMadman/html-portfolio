The Best Movies According to Min

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

This is one of my all time favorite movies because of the surreal visuals
that are also backed by scientific research. Besides the visuals, the gradual build up
of the story line really kept me on the edge of my seat through out the movie.

Comet

Another one of my favorite movies. One of the reason also being the surreal visuals,
however this movie explores all the perfect and imperfect phases in a relationship
in a very raw and realistic way.

Me and Earl and the Dying Girl

I completely forgot how I came across this movie, but I am really glad I got a chance
to watch it. It has a very unique story telling and gets me emotional everytime I watch it.
